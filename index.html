Why Us
No Guessing

Cleaning Works tailors your quote down to the square metre to guarantee you never pay a cent more than you have to for your cleaning, as we measure your site to generate your schedule and quote.

Our cleaners purchase their job from us, they are not just employees. This means they have 'ownership' of their job and are far more reliable than other cleaners. Our cleaners try harder to make you happy, as they have a financial interest in doing so.

Quality Systems

Full Compliance

Our cleaners are fully trained and inducted into your site. We also monitor their compliance requirements, such as Public Liability Insurance, OH&S Documentation, ensuring you are protected.
